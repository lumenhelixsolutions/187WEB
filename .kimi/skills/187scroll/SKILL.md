# 187SCROLL

Craft long-form scroll narratives that drive 3D camera paths, scene transitions, and progress-based reveals.

## When to use

- Scroll-driven 3D scene.
- Camera path tied to scroll progress.
- Sequenced scene reveals and transitions.

## Output

1. Scroll progress map.
2. Camera path spec.
3. Scene sequence.
4. Performance notes.
5. Next actions.

## Routes

- `187GSAP` for ScrollTrigger-driven scroll layers.
- `187HERO` when the narrative starts with an immersive hero.
- `187ACCESS+` for scroll hijacking or motion sensitivity.
