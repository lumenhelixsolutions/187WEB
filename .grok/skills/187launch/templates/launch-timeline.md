# Launch Timeline (6 Weeks)

## Week -6: Strategy

- Define ICP using [`icp-builder.md`](./icp-builder.md).
- Select 3 primary launch platforms.
- Set launch date and success metrics.

## Week -5: Assets

- Finalize landing page, pricing, and checkout.
- Produce demo video / screenshots.
- Draft all launch copy and emails.

## Week -4: Warm-Up

- Begin building in public on Twitter/LinkedIn.
- Reach out to potential hunters/amplifiers.
- Seed beta users for testimonials.

## Week -3: Product Hunt Prep

- Submit for review if required by platform.
- Confirm hunter and launch time.
- Prepare maker comment and FAQ.

## Week -2: Distribution

- Schedule social posts and newsletter sends.
- Set up analytics and UTM links.
- Brief team on launch-day roles.

## Week -1: Final Checks

- Run through [`launch-checklist.md`](./launch-checklist.md).
- Load-test site.
- Sleep.

## Week 0: Launch

- Execute launch-day blitz.
- Reply to every comment within 60 minutes.
- Track metrics in real time.

## Week +1: Follow-Up

- Thank supporters.
- Publish retrospective.
- Convert feedback into roadmap items.
