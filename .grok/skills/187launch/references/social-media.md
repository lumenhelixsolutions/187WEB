# Building in Public

## Why It Works

- **Trust:** Founders who share the journey are more credible than faceless brands.
- **Distribution:** Every post is a free ad that compounds over time.
- **Feedback:** Early community input shapes a product people actually want.

## The 80/20 Rule

- **80% value:** educational posts, behind-the-scenes lessons, useful tools.
- **20% promotion:** direct asks, launch announcements, sales links.

## Platform-Specific Playbooks

| Platform | Format | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| **Twitter/X** | Short threads, demos, metrics | 1–3× daily |
| **LinkedIn** | Long-form lessons, founder stories | 2–4× weekly |
| **Indie Hackers** | Product updates, revenue milestones | 1–2× weekly |
| **dev.to** | Technical deep-dives | 1–2× monthly |
| **YouTube / TikTok** | Demo videos, build logs | 1× weekly |

## Social Listening Setup

- Track brand mentions, competitor names, and problem keywords.
- Reply genuinely within 2 hours during launch week.
- Save recurring questions for FAQ and landing-page copy.
