# Product Hunt Launch Guide

## 4-Week Pre-Launch Checklist

- [ ] Secure a reputable hunter (or hunt yourself if you have credibility).
- [ ] Write the tagline as a thesis, not a feature list.
- [ ] Prepare 5+ high-res screenshots / a 60-second demo GIF.
- [ ] Draft the maker comment and first 10 replies to common questions.
- [ ] Warm up your audience: tweet, newsletter, LinkedIn snippets for 2 weeks.
- [ ] Set up a dedicated landing page with Product Hunt UTM tracking.

## Launch Week Blitz

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| **Monday** | Send personal notes to top supporters; confirm hunter time. |
| **Tuesday 12:01 AM PST** | Launch goes live; post maker comment within 15 minutes. |
| **Tuesday AM** | Reply to every comment within 60 minutes. |
| **Wednesday** | Share on Twitter, LinkedIn, newsletters, Slack communities. |
| **Thursday** | Push a small update or bonus based on feedback. |
| **Friday** | Thank supporters; capture metrics and testimonials. |

## Post-Launch Follow-Up (30 Days)

- Email all upvoters with a thank-you + next steps.
- Publish a launch retrospective.
- Convert top feedback into a public roadmap item.
- Follow up with journalists and newsletter writers who engaged.
