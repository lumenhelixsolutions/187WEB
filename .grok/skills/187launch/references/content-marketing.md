# Content Marketing

## Funnel Framework

| Stage | Goal | Content Type |
|---|---|---|
| **Awareness** | Capture attention | Social posts, short videos, SEO blog posts |
| **Consideration** | Build trust | Case studies, comparison pages, webinars |
| **Decision** | Convert | Demos, pricing pages, testimonials |
| **Loyalty** | Retain | Newsletters, docs, community access |

## The 20:1 Content Rule

For every 1 piece of promotional content, create 20 pieces of educational or
entertaining content that your ICP actually wants to read.

## Distribution Channels

- Owned: blog, newsletter, YouTube, podcast.
- Earned: guest posts, PR, podcast appearances, community shares.
- Paid: social ads, newsletter sponsorships, retargeting.
