# Pricing & CRO

## Pricing Model Selection

| Model | Best For | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| **Freemium** | Viral/user-generated products | High support load |
| **Usage-based** | APIs, infrastructure | Revenue volatility |
| **Tiered SaaS** | Most B2B/B2C SaaS | Complexity |
| **One-time** | Tools, templates, info products | No recurring revenue |

## Landing Page Optimization

- One clear hero thesis above the fold.
- One primary CTA; secondary CTA for skeptics.
- Social proof within first 2 scrolls.
- Pricing anchoring: show the most popular plan first.
- Remove navigation on paid landing pages.

## A/B Testing Priority

1. Headline / thesis
2. CTA copy and color
3. Pricing presentation
4. Social proof placement
5. Form length

## Conversion Heuristics

- Reduce friction: fewer fields, SSO, no credit-card trial.
- Increase urgency: limited beta seats, countdown for launch pricing.
- Reduce anxiety: security badges, refund policy, live chat.
