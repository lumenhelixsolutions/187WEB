# Launch Platforms Directory

## General Directories

| Platform | Best For | Prep | Community | Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Product Hunt** | All products | 2–4 weeks | 1M+ monthly | Get a hunter; launch Tuesday 12:01 AM PST; tell a story. |
| **Hacker News (Show HN)** | Tech/dev products | 1–2 days | 10M+ monthly | Show your work; be transparent; engage with comments. |
| **Betalist** | Early-stage SaaS | 1–2 weeks | 50k+ newsletter | Simple description; clear value prop; link to landing page. |
| **Indie Hackers** | Bootstrapped SaaS | 1–2 weeks | 100k+ community | Share revenue + journey; be authentic. |
| **Peerlist** | Developer tools | 1–2 weeks | 100k+ members | Showcase project; engage with community. |
| **dev.to** | Developer-focused | 1 week | 1M+ developers | Write a technical deep-dive about how you built it. |
| **G2 / Capterra** | B2B SaaS | 1–2 months | 5M+ monthly | Get early reviews; respond to all feedback. |
