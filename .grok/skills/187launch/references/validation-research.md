# Validation & Research

## Problem-Interview Script

1. Tell me about the last time you faced **[problem]**.
2. What did you try? Why did it fall short?
3. What would an ideal solution look like?
4. Have you budgeted for solving this? If not, why?
5. Can I follow up when we have a prototype?

## Smoke-Test Experiments

| Experiment | Validates | Success Metric |
|---|---|---|
| Landing page + waitlist | Demand | Email conversion ≥ 10% |
| Concierge MVP | Willingness to pay | 3+ paid manual deliveries |
| Fake-door test | Feature interest | Click-through rate ≥ 5% |
| Crowdfunding pre-sale | Purchase intent | Hit minimum funding goal |

## Signals You Are Ready to Build

- 10+ people describe the problem in the same words.
- 3+ people have already spent money trying to solve it.
- You can articulate the ideal customer in one sentence.
- A landing-page smoke test converts ≥ 10% of visitors.

## Signals to Pivot

- People say the problem is "nice to have" not "must have."
- They will not commit time or email for a waitlist.
- Existing solutions are "good enough."
