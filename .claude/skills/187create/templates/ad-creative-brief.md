# Ad Creative Brief

Generate **three** ad-creative variants for **[channel]** promoting **[offer]**.

## Per variant

- **Headline:** attention-grabbing, benefit-led (≤10 words).
- **Hook:** one-sentence opener that stops the scroll.
- **Visual direction:** image, video, or graphic treatment.
- **CTA:** channel-appropriate action.
- **Audience / placement:** who sees it and where.

## Variant set goals

- [ ] Each variant tests a distinct message or angle.
- [ ] Copy fits the channel's format and tone.
- [ ] Disclosures and compliance notes included where required.
- [ ] UTM or tracking labels specified.
