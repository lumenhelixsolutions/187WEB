# Community Safety Plan

Use this template for codes of conduct, forum rules, support workflows, and harassment-escalation procedures.

## Purpose

State the goal of the community space and who it serves.

## Expected behavior

- Treat every person with respect.
- Use stated names and pronouns.
- Listen to feedback about harm, even when unintended.
- Respect privacy and consent.
- Share knowledge generously and credit sources.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Deadnaming, misgendering, or deliberate use of wrong pronouns.
- Harassment, threats, slurs, or intimidation.
- Discrimination based on gender, sexuality, race, disability, religion, age, class, or nationality.
- Doxxing or sharing private identity information without consent.
- Sustained disruption or bad-faith debate.
- Tokenism, tone policing, or respectability demands.

## Response workflow

1. **Acknowledge** the report within the stated response time.
2. **Assess** safety and urgency.
3. **Document** facts without asking the reporter to over-explain their identity.
4. **Act** using the graduated response scale.
5. **Follow up** with the reporter on the outcome, to the extent privacy allows.
6. **Review** patterns quarterly to improve policy.

## Graduated responses

| Severity | Examples | Response |
|---|---|---|
| Minor | One-time misgendering corrected quickly | Private reminder, resource link |
| Moderate | Repeated microaggressions, public misgendering | Warning, temporary restriction, mandatory review |
| Severe | Harassment, slurs, doxxing | Immediate removal, ban, legal/security escalation |

## Identity-safe support language

- "Thank you for letting us know."
- "I want to make sure we refer to you correctly. What name and pronouns should I use?"
- "We take this seriously and will follow up by [time]."
- "You do not need to prove your identity to us."
