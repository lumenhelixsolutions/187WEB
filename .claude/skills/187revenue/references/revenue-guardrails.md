# Revenue Guardrails

Hard rules for all 187REVENUE outputs.

## Prohibited

- Fake coupons or expired deals presented as active.
- Undisclosed affiliate links, referral kickbacks, or sponsored placements.
- Fake reviews, testimonials, or social proof.
- Fake scarcity timers, false inventory claims, or counterfeit goods.
- Misleading shipping times or fulfillment claims.
- Hidden recurring billing or unexpected rebills.
- Dropshipping products without verified suppliers and sample checks.
- Health, disability, financial, legal, or revenue guarantees without evidence and human review.

## Required

- Affiliate and referral disclosures visible before the user acts.
- Accurate coupon validity, expiration, and restrictions.
- Refund, chargeback, and cancellation policies published before checkout.
- Accessibility review for checkout, pricing, and deal pages.
- Legal/tax review gate for regulated products, regions, or business models.
- Clear distinction between proved, measured, modeled, and speculative revenue claims.

## Evidence labels

When stating revenue outcomes, label each claim:

| Label | Meaning |
|---|---|
| proved | Replicated outcome with records |
| measured | Directly tracked metric |
| modeled | Calculation based on assumptions |
| interpreted | Reasonable inference from data |
| speculative | Possible but unverified |

## Review checklist

- [ ] Affiliate disclosures present and clear
- [ ] Coupon/deal freshness verified
- [ ] Supplier verification documented (dropshipping)
- [ ] Refund and chargeback policy published
- [ ] Recurring billing disclosed
- [ ] Accessibility review passed
- [ ] Legal/tax review completed (if regulated)
- [ ] No prohibited claims or guarantees
