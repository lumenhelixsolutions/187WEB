# Compliance Review Gate

Checkpoint before any revenue system launches.

## When to invoke

- Selling physical goods, digital products, or services.
- Processing payments in regulated regions.
- Offering subscriptions, trials, or auto-renewals.
- Running affiliate, coupon, or deal programs.
- Dropshipping or third-party fulfillment.
- Health, wellness, financial, legal, disability, or education adjacent products.

## Region checklist

| Region | Key considerations |
|---|---|
| United States | State sales tax nexus, FTC disclosure rules, PCI DSS |
| European Union | GDPR, VAT OSS, Consumer Rights Directive, PCI DSS |
| United Kingdom | UK GDPR, VAT, Consumer Contracts Regulations |
| Canada | GST/HST, PIPEDA, provincial consumer law |
| Australia | GST, ACL consumer guarantees, Privacy Act |
| Global | Local tax, currency, dispute, and accessibility laws |

## Required documents

- Refund and cancellation policy.
- Terms of service.
- Privacy policy covering payment data.
- Affiliate/referral disclosure (if applicable).
- Subscription terms including auto-renewal and cancellation.

## Human review gates

- Legal review for regulated claims.
- Tax professional for nexus and registration.
- Accessibility review for checkout and pricing pages.
- Finance review for margin and chargeback risk.

## Output

A signed-off compliance checklist stored in the project vault or repo before launch.
