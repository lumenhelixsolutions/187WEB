# Plain Language Checklist

Checklist for 187WRITE plain-language rewrites.

## Sentence structure

- [ ] Average sentence length is 15–20 words.
- [ ] No sentence exceeds 25 words without a clear reason.
- [ ] One idea per sentence.

## Word choice

- [ ] Common words replace jargon.
- [ ] Technical terms are defined on first use.
- [ ] Acronyms are spelled out on first use.
- [ ] Active voice is used for instructions.

## Organization

- [ ] The goal is stated first.
- [ ] Steps are numbered.
- [ ] Related options are bulleted.
- [ ] Paragraphs stay under 5 sentences.

## Reader orientation

- [ ] Uses "you" to address the reader.
- [ ] Explains why a step matters.
- [ ] Highlights key takeaways.

## Accessibility

- [ ] Headings are descriptive and hierarchical.
- [ ] Tables have header rows.
- [ ] Link text describes the destination.
- [ ] No essential information relies on color alone.

## Target scores

| Audience | Flesch Reading Ease | Grade level |
|---|---|---|
| General public | 60–70 | 8–9 |
| Developers | 40–60 | 10–12 |
| Researchers | 30–50 | 12–14 |

Route identity-sensitive rewrites to `187include` and disability-access rewrites to `187access-plus`.
