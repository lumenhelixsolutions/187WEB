## Formatting

- Headings: sentence case.
- Lists: numbered for sequences, bullets for options.
- Code: syntax-highlighted, tested or marked `needs verification`.
- Links: descriptive text, not "click here".

## Pronouns and identity

- Default to singular "they" for unknown individuals.
- Use stated names and pronouns.
- Avoid binary gender assumptions in examples.

## Claim discipline

- Label evidence level for every public claim.
- Flag high-risk claims for expert review.
- Avoid absolute guarantees.
