# Ads & Influencers

## Paid Ads Starter Stack

| Platform | Best For | Budget Signal |
|---|---|---|
| **Google Search** | High-intent keywords | $$$ |
| **Meta (FB/IG)** | B2C, lookalike audiences | $$ |
| **LinkedIn** | B2B, precise job targeting | $$$$ |
| **Twitter/X** | Developer/founder audiences | $$ |
| **Reddit** | Niche communities | $ |

## Influencer Identification

- Search the platform where your ICP spends time.
- Look for creators with 5k–50k engaged followers (not just raw reach).
- Check comment quality and recent sponsorship density.
- Negotiate performance-based or affiliate deals when possible.

## Affiliate Program Basics

- Offer 20–30% recurring commission for SaaS.
- Provide unique tracking links and swipe copy.
- Pay monthly and spotlight top affiliates publicly.

## Budget Rule

Start with **$500–$1,000** to test 2–3 channels. Kill losers within 7 days and
double down on positive ROAS.
