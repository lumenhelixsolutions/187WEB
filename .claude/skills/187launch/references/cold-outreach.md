# Cold Outreach

## ICP Builder

Use [`templates/icp-builder.md`](../templates/icp-builder.md) to define:

- Job titles and seniority
- Pain points and current workarounds
- Where they hang out online
- Decision triggers and buying committees

## Cold Email Principles

1. **One idea per email.**
2. **Personalize the first line** with a real observation.
3. **Lead with the problem, not the product.**
4. **Ask for a small yes** (feedback, 10-minute call).
5. **Follow up 3–4 times** over 2 weeks.

## Follow-Up Sequence

| Touch | Timing | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Day 1 | Intro + value hypothesis |
| 2 | Day 3 | Share relevant social proof |
| 3 | Day 7 | Ask a specific question |
| 4 | Day 12 | Break-up email + resource link |

## Response Rate Optimization

- Keep emails under 100 words.
- Send Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10 AM recipient timezone.
- Use a plain-text look; avoid heavy HTML.
- Track opens/replies and A/B test subject lines.
