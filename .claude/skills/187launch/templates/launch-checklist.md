# Launch Day Checklist

## Pre-Launch (T-7 Days)

- [ ] Pricing page live and tested.
- [ ] Analytics and conversion tracking verified.
- [ ] Support channel ready (email, chat, Discord).
- [ ] Press kit + screenshots uploaded.
- [ ] Email announcement drafted and scheduled.
- [ ] Social queue scheduled for launch day.
- [ ] Team roles assigned (who replies, who fixes, who posts).

## Launch Day

- [ ] Site is live and load-tested.
- [ ] Primary launch post goes live at planned time.
- [ ] Maker/founder comment posted within 15 minutes.
- [ ] Replies to comments/questions within 60 minutes.
- [ ] Cross-post to Twitter, LinkedIn, relevant communities.
- [ ] Monitor uptime and error logs.

## Post-Launch (T+1 to T+7)

- [ ] Send thank-you email to early supporters.
- [ ] Collect testimonials and feedback.
- [ ] Publish launch retrospective.
- [ ] Update roadmap based on feedback.
- [ ] Begin outreach to press and newsletters.
