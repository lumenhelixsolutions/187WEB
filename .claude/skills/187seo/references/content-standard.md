# 187SEO Content Standard

## E-E-A-T

Content must demonstrate:

- **Experience:** First-hand knowledge, case studies, or original research when relevant.
- **Expertise:** Authored or reviewed by subject-matter experts.
- **Authoritativeness:** Credible sources, internal and external links to reputable references.
- **Trustworthiness:** Clear about us, contact, sources, dates, and correction policies.

## Intent matching

- Identify the intent behind each target query: informational, navigational, commercial, transactional.
- Structure content to satisfy that intent faster than competing pages.
- Use headings, summaries, tables, lists, and media that match expected formats.

## Content brief structure

A 187SEO content brief must include:

1. Objective and success metric
2. Target audience and persona
3. Primary and secondary keywords
4. Search intent
5. Suggested outline and headings
6. Unique angle or differentiator
7. Internal linking opportunities
8. Required media or schema
9. Competitor references
10. Publication and refresh cadence

## Originality and freshness

- Avoid regurgitated content. Add original insight, data, examples, or tools.
- Update evergreen content regularly based on performance and topic changes.
- Remove or consolidate outdated, duplicate, or thin pages.

## Accessibility of content

- Use descriptive headings in logical order (H1 → H2 → H3).
- Write alt text for informative images.
- Provide transcripts or captions for video/audio.
- Ensure readable color contrast and font sizing.
- Make interactive content keyboard accessible.
