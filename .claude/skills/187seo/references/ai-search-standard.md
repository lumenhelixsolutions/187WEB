# 187SEO AI Search Standard (AEO / GEO)

## Answer-engine optimization (AEO)

- Provide direct, concise answers to explicit user questions.
- Use clear headings, definitions, and step-by-step instructions.
- Structure content with FAQ, HowTo, and Q&A schema where appropriate.
- Place the answer near the top, then expand with depth and nuance.

## Generative-engine optimization (GEO)

- Improve entity clarity: state who you are, what you do, and why it matters in plain language.
- Use semantic markup and consistent terminology across pages.
- Include credible sources, citations, and data to increase likelihood of being referenced.
- Optimize for topical authority by covering related subtopics thoroughly.

## Source citations

- Link to original research, authoritative references, and official documentation.
- Avoid unsourced claims, especially for YMYL (Your Money or Your Life) topics.
- Maintain a references or sources section for data-heavy content.

## Controlling hallucinated summaries

- Keep page messaging consistent so AI summaries cannot combine contradictory statements.
- Use clear, unambiguous language around key facts, pricing, capabilities, and limitations.
- Monitor brand mentions and AI-generated citations using available tools and manual spot checks.

## Policy alignment

- Do not create content solely to manipulate AI overviews.
- Avoid clickbait framing, false urgency, or misrepresentation designed to game summarization.
- Follow the same E-E-A-T and spam-policy standards that apply to traditional SEO.
